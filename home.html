War Card Game App

Crafted using Xcode, this iOS app offers a streamlined experience on a single page. Upon pressing the 'deal' button, the app reveals two cards—one for the player and one for the computer. The higher card takes the win, and points are tallied accordingly. It's a straightforward and engaging card game experience.

Guidebook App

This guidebook app serves as your digital companion for multiple cities, offering insightful descriptions to help you explore. Simply select a city, and you'll be presented with a list of potential landmarks waiting to be discovered. Dive deeper into each landmark's story by tapping on your chosen destination, where you'll find detailed descriptions and enriching information to enhance your journey.
